Zerdesigns Studio

My name is Zer McBride. When you look at one of the images that I've presented today, I believe it is an extension of who I am as a photographer. Having a lifelong fascination with light, studying it through photography is my true passion. I try to capture the natural and man-made world that I see.

I use photography as a form of self-expression. My choice of subject comes from many different places and angles. My work is deeply personal and is not driven by any commercial agenda but is created and delivered from the heart. It's my world and you're welcome to it!
